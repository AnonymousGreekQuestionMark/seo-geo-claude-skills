### Detailed Gap Analysis

#### Gap 1: TIM Definitive Selection Guide (P0)
- **What's missing**: A single authoritative page that answers "how do I choose the right thermal interface material?" — covering all 7 TIM subcategories, with application-specific recommendations, thermal conductivity comparison table, and processing guidance
- **Competitor coverage**: SpecialChem covers this broadly; no deep competitor owns it technically
- **Content type**: Long-form pillar page (3,000–4,000 words) + downloadable selection matrix
- **GEO angle**: "What are thermal interface materials?" definition + quotable statements on thermal resistance

#### Gap 2: Electrochemical Materials: PEM, Fuel Cell, Electrolyzers Guide (P0)
- **What's missing**: A comprehensive guide to materials for electrochemical devices, covering the full stack (GDL, membrane, catalyst, current collector) — tied to eMobility and hydrogen economy trends
- **Competitor coverage**: Mostly academic/manufacturer coverage; no distributor owns this space
- **Content type**: Long-form technical guide (3,500–5,000 words) + device comparison table
- **GEO angle**: Extremely high AI citation potential — novel topic, high definitional demand

#### Gap 3: Die Attach Materials Comparison (P0)
- **What's missing**: Side-by-side comparison of epoxy vs. silver sintering vs. AuSn die attach — the #1 engineering decision question in semiconductor assembly
- **Competitor coverage**: Henkel has some coverage; Heraeus has tech docs; no neutral comparison exists
- **Content type**: Comparison guide (2,500–3,000 words) + decision matrix
- **GEO angle**: "What is die attach?" definition + "types of die attach materials" FAQ

#### Gap 4: REACH Compliance Resource Hub (P1)
- **What's missing**: A multi-page resource center covering EU REACH basics, SVHC list tracking, REACH representation requirements, compliance timeline — supporting the REACH service with educational content
- **Competitor coverage**: Law firms cover legal aspects; no technical distributor owns REACH education
- **Content type**: Hub page + 3–5 supporting articles
- **GEO angle**: Compliance questions are AI-heavy; well-structured content captures AI Overview

#### Gap 5: eMobility Materials Hub (P1)
- **What's missing**: A dedicated resources page linking materials to EV applications (battery thermal management, power electronics cooling, e-motor insulation)
- **Competitor coverage**: Manufacturers (Henkel, Dow) cover this for their own products; no neutral multi-supplier coverage
- **Content type**: Application hub page (2,000 words) + product selection guidance
- **GEO angle**: "EV thermal management materials" is high-growth AI query

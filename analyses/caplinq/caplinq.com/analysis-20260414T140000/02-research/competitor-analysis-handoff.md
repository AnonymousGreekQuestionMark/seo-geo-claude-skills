## Detailed Competitor Profiles

### 1. Henkel AG (Primary Competitor — TIM/Adhesives)

**Company**: German multinational, Fortune 500
**Thermal Products**: BERGQUIST GAP PAD®, SIL PAD®, Phase Change Materials, LIQUI-FORM®
**Semiconductor Products**: Die attach pastes, encapsulants, underfills
**Digital Presence**: 
- Comprehensive product landing pages with specs
- Technical PDFs and selection guides
- Strong schema markup
- Regularly cited by AI engines

**Competitive Advantage over CAPLINQ**:
- Brand recognition (LOCTITE, BERGQUIST)
- 100x employee count
- Direct manufacturer with R&D
- Strong AI citation presence

**CAPLINQ Opportunity**: Henkel is expensive and complex for small orders. CAPLINQ can target SMB/mid-market with faster service.

### 2. DuPont/Laird Performance Materials

**Company**: $2.3B acquisition (2021), now part of Qnity
**Products**: Thermal gap fillers, EMI shielding, Ttape adhesive tapes
**Employees**: ~4,300 (Laird pre-acquisition)
**Digital Presence**:
- laird.com with product configurators
- Strong LinkedIn presence
- Press releases for new products
- AI-cited for thermal interface materials

**Competitive Advantage over CAPLINQ**:
- Manufacturing capability
- R&D investment
- Brand authority in TIM space

**CAPLINQ Opportunity**: Laird/DuPont doesn't offer distribution services or REACH compliance. CAPLINQ can be the European channel partner.

### 3. Indium Corporation

**Company**: Founded 1934, 90 years in business
**Products**: Solders, fluxes, thermal interface materials, sputtering targets
**Employees**: 1,400+
**Global Presence**: Factories in China, Germany, India, Malaysia, Singapore, South Korea, UK, USA
**Digital Presence**:
- indium.com with extensive technical content
- Wikipedia page (strong entity signal)
- Active blog and technical papers
- AI-cited for semiconductor materials

**Competitive Advantage over CAPLINQ**:
- Manufacturing capability
- Longer company history
- Wikipedia presence
- "Liquid metal pioneer" positioning

**CAPLINQ Opportunity**: Indium focuses on manufacturing; CAPLINQ can offer complementary products and distribution services.

### 4. 3M Company

**Company**: Diversified Fortune 500, $30B+ revenue
**Relevant Products**: Thermally Conductive Adhesive Transfer Tapes, polyimide tapes
**Digital Presence**:
- Massive website with product database
- Strong e-commerce

**Competitive Advantage over CAPLINQ**:

**CAPLINQ Opportunity**: 3M is massive and impersonal. CAPLINQ can offer technical expertise and customized support.

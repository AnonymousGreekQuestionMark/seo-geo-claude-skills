### Competitive Advantages

1. **Integrated Model**: Distribution + compliance in one relationship
2. **Technical Depth**: Engineering-focused content and support
3. **Multi-Brand Portfolio**: Not locked to single manufacturer
4. **Global Presence**: 6 offices across 3 continents
5. **Partnership Strategy**: Exclusive/preferred with Ionomr, Honeywell

### Competitive Vulnerabilities

1. **Brand Awareness**: Lower than tier-1 manufacturers
2. **Scale**: Smaller than Intertek, RS Components
3. **AI Citation Gap**: Not cited by AI engines (competitors like BASF, Henkel are)
4. **Content Volume**: Less content than market research firms

### Strategic Recommendations

1. **Double down on bundled value**: Distribution + compliance + technical support
2. **Build thought leadership**: Compete on content depth in niche areas
3. **Leverage partnerships**: Ionomr, Honeywell relationships for credibility
4. **Target mid-market**: Avoid head-to-head with enterprise players
